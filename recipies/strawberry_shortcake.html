Strawberry ShortCake

Strawberry shortcake is super tasty, yay yay hurray

Ingredients:
  • 1 quart strawberries (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 12 ounce sponge cake dessert cups
  • 1 7 ounce can whipped cream
Instructions:
  1. Place strawberries in bowl
  2. Add sugar in bowl
  3. Mix sugar and strawberries
  4. Let goodness ensue
